Book Detail and Book Review

From the book search page, or other pages that contains a sketch of a book, user can always click the "new-window" button to open the book's detail page.

  • Book Detail Information: The details page shows fine-grained information of the selected book on the top, and user can "favorite" the book by clicking "Favorite" button under the thumbnail of the book.

  • Review Section: Under the book information is a list of book reviews for book, and a review form where user can submit their own reviews for the book. Notice that only user already logged in can submit a review. Otherwise they will be redirected to log in page.

  • User Recommend Section: At the bottom of the book detail page, a list of user and the tile of their favorite books is displayed. So user can find other users with similar reading taste, and checkout their profiles and add them as friends at their profile page.
