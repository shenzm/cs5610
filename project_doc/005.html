Favorite Books and Follow Users

  • Favorite Books: In ReadiBook, user can favorite books and follow other users, for their future usage. For example, user can favorite a book at any page that has a sketch of a book. It can be a book search result, a book detail page. And user can find interesting books by just looking at their friends favorite book collection.

  • Follow Friends: At mentioned before, the app will recommend a list of users at the bottom of the book detail page who have already favorite the book. So this would be a chance for users to find other similar taste readers. And after adding a user as friend, they can browser their friends follow list, in which may be some other user that shares the same interest with them. So this chain of friends are really useful to find interesting book to read.
